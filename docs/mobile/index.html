Mobile Libraries

Stripe has native libraries to let you accept payments in your iOS and Android applications. If you need help after reading this, check out our answers to common questions or chat live with other developers in #stripe on freenode.

Our native libraries for Android and iOS lets you collect credit card information without having to deal with sensitive data passing through your servers.

iOS

Our iOS libraries let you easily collect and process your customers' payment information. They also seamlessly integrate with Apple Pay, Apple's integrated payment framework. They remain backwards-compatible to iOS 5.0.

  • View guide
  • View on GitHub
  • View on Fabric

Android

We support Android from version 4 and up. The library also comes with a set of validation helpers to help you build payment forms.

Mobile Web

Checkout supports Mobile Safari and Android's browser with a specially customized flow for mobile devices. Once you've included Checkout on your website, customers on mobile devices will automatically see a tailored experience. Learn more
